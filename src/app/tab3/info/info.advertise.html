Advertise

TV advertisements target a specific group of states. You can gain 1-2% in each of the states in the group, making this a great tool for winning an election. In order to afford an advertisement, you need to fundraise.

The groups of states that you can advertise to are:


HI, CA, WA, OR

IL, MN

NM, CO, NV, AZ

ME, NH, VA, PA

WI, IA, MI, OH

FL, NC, GA

TX, AK

SC, MO, IN, MS, LA
