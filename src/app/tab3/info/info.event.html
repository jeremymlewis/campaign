Event

Special Events happen every other round! A total of 8 will occur during a regular game. During a special event, you draw an event card and it has a random effect on the game.

Some events are based on regional issues and will affect a few states, while some events will affect the entire country. Some events ask you to answer a question... and see how the public responds to your answer.

Many of the events are based on real events from history! Read the 'fun facts' to learn more about US history.
